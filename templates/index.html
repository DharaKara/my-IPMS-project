Comprehensive Car Insurance

This provides cover for just about any form of financial loss related to your vehicle, including car accidents, thefts, hijackings, fire damage, natural disasters, and damage to someone else's car.

Learn More About Comprehensive Car Insurance
Third Party, Fire & Theft Insurance

If you don't want the full package of a comprehensive car insurance policy, but still want some form of cover, then third-party, fire & theft is a good option. This will pay for the accidental damage you might cause if you're at fault in a car accident. It will also protect you financially if your car is damaged by a fire, stolen, or if you're hijacked.

Learn More About Third-Party, Fire & Theft Insurance
Third Party Car Insurance

Want to be financially covered in case you cause an accident? This is the type of insurance you want. It will ensure that you aren't paying for repairs on someone else's car completely out of your own pocket if you cause a road accident.
